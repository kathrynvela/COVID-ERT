Review Resources

Organizations

Centre for Evidence-Based Medicine

The CEBM has committed its skills and expertise in evidence synthesis and dissemination to the effort against the current COVID-19 pandemic.
EPPI-Centre - COVID-19: Living Systematic Map of the Evidence

A living map of studies on COVID-19, identified in MEDLINE and Embase, and published in 2019 or later.
Evidence Aid - Coronavirus (COVID-19): Evidence Collection

Evidence Aid is preparing summaries of relevant research related to COVID-19.
Cochrane Rapid Reviews

Cochrane COVID Rapid Reviews seek to provide rapid evidence-based information and has engaged with WHO and other stakeholders to build a bank of questions.
National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE)

A collection of rapid guidelines and evidence summaries related to COVID-19.
EBSCO COVID-19 Updates and Information

A collection of COVID-19 guidelines, searches, and news updates curated by EBSCO.
ECRI COVID-19 Resource Center

A landing page for ECRI reviews and guidelines related to COVID-19.

Databases

LitCovid (NIH/NLM)

A curated literature hub for tracking studies about COVID-19, providing central access to relevant articles in PubMed, hosted by NIH/NLM.
Global research on coronavirus disease (COVID-19) (WHO)

A database of publications on COVID-19.
medRxiv

A collection of COVID-19 preprints from medRxiv and bioRxiv.
Outbreak Science Rapid PREreview

A web application for open, rapid reviews of outbreak-related preprints.
Covid-19 Open Research Dataset (CORD-19)

A free, open resource generated by the Allen Institute for AI.

Clinical Characterization and Management
Potential Rapid Diagnostics, Vaccine and Therapeutics for 2019 Novel Coronavirus (2019-nCoV): A Systematic Review

2/26/2020 | Pang J, Wang MX, Ang IYH, et al. | J Clin Med

Aim: To describe and assess the potential rapid diagnostics, vaccines and therapeutics for 2019-nCoV, based in part on the developments for MERS-CoV and SARS-CoV
Prevalence of comorbidities in the novel Wuhan coronavirus (COVID-19) infection: a systematic review and meta-analysis

3/12/2020 | Yang J, Zheng Y, Gou X, et al. | Int J Infect Dis

Aim: To assess the prevalence of comorbidities in the COVID-19 infection patients and the risk of underlying diseases in severe patients compared to non-severe patients.
Clinical, laboratory and imaging features of COVID-19: A systematic review and meta-analysis

03/13/2020 | Rodriguez-Morales AJ, Cardona-Ospina JA, Gutiérrez-Ocampo E, et al. | Travel Med Infect Dis

Aim: To summarize the clinical, laboratory, and image features of COVID-19; to examine the outcome of COVID-19 cases, including risk factors, the proportion of patients requiring ICU and those with fatal outcomes; and to assess the prevalence of comorbidities among COVID-19 confirmed cases.
Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19): A Systematic Review of Imaging Findings in 919 Patients

3/14/2020 | Salehi S, Abedi A, Balakrishnan S, Gholamrezanezhad A. | AJR Am J Roentgenol

Aim: To identify the CT characteristics of this life-threatening and rapidly spreading viral pneumonia, summarize key imaging findings in different stages of the disease, and define the evolution of CT findings associated with disease progression or clinical improvement. We also aim to investigate how CT findings correlate with patients’ age, severity of clinical symptoms, laboratory testing, and disease prognosis.
Coronavirus in pregnancy and delivery: rapid review

03/18/2020 | Mullins E, Evans D, Viner RM, et al. | Ultrasound Obstet Gynecol

Aim: To guide treatment and prevention in women affected by COVID-19 during pregnancy in the current outbreak.
COVID-19 and Smoking: A Systematic Review of the Evidence

03/20/2020 | Vardavas CI, Nikitara K | Tob Induc Dis

What is the assocation between smoking and COVID-19 outcomes including the severity of the disease, the need for mechanical ventilation, the need for intensive care unite (ICU) hosptialization, and death?
Systematic Review of COVID-19 in Children Show Milder Cases and a Better Prognosis than Adults

03/23/2020 | Ludvigsson JF | Acta Paediatr

What is the evidence on COVID-19 incidence and disease course in children?
COVID-19 Computed Tomography Findings: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis

3/25/2020 | Bao C, Liu X, Zhang H, et al. | J Am Coll Radiol

Aim: To provide a more precise estimate of detection of COVID-19 by chest CT and report on the most common imaging findings on chest CT imaging.
Corticosteroid Therapy for COVID-19 Infection

3/27/2020 | Helfand M

Aim: To review the evidence for using corticosteroids to treat acute respiratory distress syndrome in pateints with COVID-19.
